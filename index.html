My Recipies


Looking for healthy dinner ideas? We combed the site for the three recipes that have it all -- crave worthy flavor, healthy ingredients, and simple preparations. These delicious main dish recipes are loaded with healthy whole foods. They feature lots of veggies, lean proteins, and healthy fats like olive oil.

Spiralized Cucumber and Jicama Tostada Salad with Avocado

Spiralized jicama, cucumber, red onion, and jalapeno peppers combine with sweet bell peppers and grape tomatoes atop a creamy avocado spread. "Change up the veggies or the dressing to suit your taste," suggests bd.weld.

Grilled Chicken Fajita Salad

Grilled marinated strips of skinless, boneless chicken breast top romaine lettuce, red bell peppers, and onion. "Garnish this low-carb, no-sweat, sizzling-hot grilled fajita salad with lime wedges and chopped cilantro," says Baking Nana.
